Request Account Deletion — Kibank

We respect your right to control your data. You can request deletion of your Kibank account and all associated data at any time.

How to request account deletion:
  1. Send an email to contact@intl-coltium.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address registered to your Kibank account
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What gets deleted

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your profile photo
  • All inventory items and product images
  • All sales records and transaction history
  • All savings plans and Senti Pot data

What is retained

  • Nothing — all data is permanently deleted within 30 days of your request

If you have questions, email us at contact@intl-coltium.com
